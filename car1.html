About the Toyota Supra

The Toyota Supra is a legendary sports car known for its iconic design, powerful engine, and exceptional performance. It has a rich history, beloved by car enthusiasts and racers alike.

Generations of the Supra

  • Mark I (1978–1981)
  • Mark II (1981–1986)
  • Mark III (1986–1993)
  • Mark IV (1993–2002)
  • Mark V (2019–Present)

Top Features of the Supra

  1. Turbocharged inline-six engines
  2. Aerodynamic body design
  3. Advanced suspension system
  4. Luxurious yet sporty interiors
  5. Cutting-edge technology

Key Terms

Turbocharger
A device that increases an engine's efficiency and power output by forcing extra air into the combustion chamber.
Inline-Six Engine
An engine with six cylinders arranged in a single row.
Aerodynamics
The study of how air moves around objects, crucial for car speed and stability.
